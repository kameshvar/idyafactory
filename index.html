Welcome to idyafactory

idyafactory (pronounced as idea factory) is a startup recognized by the Department of Industrial Policy & Promotion, Government of India, under the StartupIndia program. idyafactory has a bouquet of highly innovative and differentiated products for the dematerialized world, serving key verticals such as education, healthcare, space & hi-tech and retail, transforming the way businesses think and run, deploying technology for mass reach, taking technology solutions to the under privileged, democratizing reach and availability of knowledge and information across the globe augmenting business and individual decision-making ability. idyafactory strongly believes in embracing the innovation tsunami, democratizing ideation and industrializing innovations for business benefits. idyafactory ushers in a paradigm shift in the way the businesses are conducted by transforming their processes in a highly innovative manner, deploying its product and services for global customers. idyafactory intends to realize its product dreams using technologies including but not limited to AI, AR/VR, Internet of Things (IOT) making use of analytics, social media, mobile and cloud delivery platforms.
